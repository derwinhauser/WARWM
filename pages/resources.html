WARWM Resources

WARWM disseminates the latest workplace mobbing research insights through the Journal of Workplace Mobbing, conference proceedings, dedicated podcasts, quarterly webinars, educational videos, and more. To support this ongoing research, WARWM also provides grants and scholarships for research proposals. Members can join the WARWM social media sites (LinkedIn) to stay connected.
