Membership Fee Waiver Policy

WARWM understands that workplace mobbing can disrupt careers and finances. If you've been impacted by mobbing and are currently unable to pay the membership fee, you may apply for a full fee waiver. Approved applicants receive all WARWM benefits for free, and your circumstances are kept completely confidential.

Apply or learn more: membership@warwm.org
