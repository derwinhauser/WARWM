Travel Information

Destination: Niagara Falls, New York

Niagara Falls is one of the most recognized international meeting locations in North America. The city offers convenient access by air, rail, and road, and provides a unique setting for academic exchange and collaboration.

Arriving by Air

Buffalo Niagara International Airport (BUF) - Closest major airport, approximately 25-30 miles (40-50 minutes) from Niagara Falls

  • Direct flights from many major U.S. cities
  • International connections through major hubs
  • Ground transportation: taxis, rideshare, rental cars, hotel shuttles

Toronto Pearson International Airport (YYZ) - Located approximately 1.5-2 hours from Niagara Falls

Arriving by Train

Niagara Falls Amtrak Station provides regional connections through Buffalo and other major routes.

Arriving by Car

Easily reachable via Interstate 90 (I-90) and Interstate 190 (I-190)

  • From Buffalo, NY: ~40 minutes
  • From Rochester, NY: ~1.5 hours
  • From Toronto, Canada: ~1.5-2 hours (plus border crossing)
