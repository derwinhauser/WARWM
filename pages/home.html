About WARWM

The World Association for Research on Workplace Mobbing (WARWM) is an international nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing research, education, and collaboration on workplace mobbing. Through global scholarship, conferences, and publications such as the Journal of Workplace Mobbing, WARWM connects researchers and practitioners to promote healthier workplaces, accountability, and meaningful change worldwide.
